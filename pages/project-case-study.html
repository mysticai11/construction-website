Luxury Villa Estate

A custom-designed luxury residence featuring modern architecture, sustainable materials, and smart home integration.

Client Private Residence
Location Beverly Hills, CA
Duration 18 Months
Completed December 2023

Project Overview

The Luxury Villa Estate project represents the pinnacle of residential construction excellence. Our client envisioned a contemporary Mediterranean-style residence that seamlessly blends indoor and outdoor living spaces while incorporating the latest in sustainable building practices.

Working closely with renowned architects and interior designers, BuildPro delivered a 8,500 square foot masterpiece featuring 6 bedrooms, 8 bathrooms, a home theater, wine cellar, and infinity pool with panoramic city views.

Scope of Work

  • Complete design-build services from concept to completion
  • Custom foundation engineering for hillside construction
  • Structural steel and reinforced concrete framework
  • High-end finishes including Italian marble and hardwood flooring
  • Smart home automation and security systems
  • Landscape design with drought-resistant native plants
  • Solar panel installation and Tesla Powerwall integration

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: The hillside location presented significant engineering challenges, including unstable soil conditions and strict local building codes for seismic safety.

Solution: Our team employed advanced geo-technical analysis and designed a custom deep foundation system with caissons extending 40 feet into bedrock. We worked closely with city inspectors throughout the process, exceeding all seismic requirements by 25%.

Challenge: The client requested a tight timeline to coincide with their relocation schedule, requiring accelerated construction without compromising quality.

Solution: We implemented a fast-track construction methodology with overlapping phases and prefabricated components. Daily coordination meetings and real-time project tracking kept all stakeholders aligned, ultimately delivering the project 2 weeks ahead of schedule.

Project Outcome

The Luxury Villa Estate was completed on schedule and 3% under budget. The property has since been featured in Architectural Digest and received the 2024 Excellence in Residential Design award from the California Building Industry Association.
