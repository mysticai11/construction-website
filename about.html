Our Story

From Garage to General Contractor

In 2008, Tom Mitchell started Apex Construction with one truck, a commitment to quality, and a belief that construction didn't have to be stressful.

What began as small renovation jobs has grown into a full-service commercial and industrial contractor. Today, we're still guided by the same principles: do what you say, communicate honestly, and build things that last.

We're not trying to be the biggest contractor in the Midwest. We focus on projects where we can make a real difference‚Äîdelivering personal attention that larger firms can't match.
