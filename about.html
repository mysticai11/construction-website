A Legacy of Excellence

Since 1979, Apex Infrastructure Group has delivered critical infrastructure and landmark projects across the globe.

Our Story

What began as a regional construction firm in Houston has grown into one of the world's most respected infrastructure partners.

Founded by James Morrison Sr. in 1979, Apex Infrastructure Group has spent over four decades building a reputation for excellence, safety, and reliability. Today, we operate across 18 countries, delivering projects that shape communities and economies around the world.

Our success is built on a simple principle: deliver exceptional work, every time. This commitment has earned the trust of governments, Fortune 500 companies, and institutional clients who rely on us for their most critical projects.
