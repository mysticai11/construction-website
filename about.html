History

Four Decades of Execution

Founded in 1979, Apex Infrastructure Group began as a commercial renovation practice serving central Ohio. Over four decades, we've grown into a full-service construction firm delivering complex projects across three states.

Our growth has been deliberate. We've scaled at a pace that maintains the direct oversight and accountability that set us apart from day one. Today, we manage projects from $500K to $50M+ across commercial, industrial, and government sectors.

Leadership remains hands-on. Executive oversight on every project. No layers between decision-makers and execution.
