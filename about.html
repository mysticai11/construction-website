About Apex Infrastructure

Our Company

Founded on Straightforward Principles

In 2008, Thomas Mitchell founded Apex Infrastructure with a clear objective: deliver construction projects the way clients actually want them delivered—on time, on budget, and without surprises.

What began as a small commercial renovation practice has grown into a full-service construction firm serving clients across Ohio, Indiana, and Kentucky. Today, we manage projects ranging from tenant build-outs to ground-up industrial facilities.

Our growth has been deliberate. We've scaled at a pace that allows us to maintain the hands-on project oversight and direct communication that set us apart from day one.
